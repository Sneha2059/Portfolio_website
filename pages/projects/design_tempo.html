Safa Tempo Redesign

Improving passenger comfort in Nepal's popular electric vehicle

SolidWorks CAD Design Transportation Ergonomics

Introduction

The Safa Tempo, also known as the Green Tempo, is a unique form of public transportation that has gained immense popularity in Nepal. It is a three-wheeled vehicle designed to provide affordable and environmentally friendly transportation options in urban areas, particularly in Kathmandu, the capital city.

The concept of the Safa Tempo was introduced as a solution to the growing traffic congestion and pollution problems faced by Nepal's major cities. It was envisioned as an alternative to traditional buses and taxis, offering a more compact and agile mode of transportation that can navigate through narrow streets and congested areas.

Methodology

Based on the design parameters, the 3D CAD model design of the outer body of Safa Tempo was made in SolidWorks. The design process began by creating a 3D model of the Safa Tempo in SolidWorks. The basic dimensions and overall form of the vehicle were defined based on the conceptualization phase.

Design Improvements

The design of the outer body of the Safa Tempo is of importance as it greatly influences the vehicle's aesthetics, functionality, and aerodynamic performance. In my redesigned concept, I have made significant changes to the breadth and height of the Safa Tempo. This modification was driven by the pressing issue of passenger inconvenience and discomfort experienced in the current model, particularly with limited space and frequent head bumps when standing.

The primary objective of expanding the Safa Tempo's dimensions was to create a more spacious interior, allowing passengers to move about freely without feeling cramped. By increasing the breadth, there is ample room for passengers to comfortably sit, while the heightened design prevents any potential head bumps when standing.

Also, a long rod has been added at the end of the design so that the passengers can enter and exit while holding the rod ensuring their safety.

Design Specifications

The dimensions for the redesigned model of Safa Tempo are:

  • Length: 3500 mm
  • Breadth: 1500 mm
  • Height: 2200 mm

Conclusion

The final redesigned model was made keeping in mind the passengers' capability and accessibility. The results of the redesigned Safa Tempo with increased breadth and height have been highly favorable and the modifications have successfully addressed passenger comfort and accessibility.
