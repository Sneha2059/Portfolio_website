Financial Analysis of a Real Estate Commercial Building in Kathmandu

Undergraduate Case Study | Team Project | Economic Feasibility Analysis

Project Type
Case Study Analysis
Duration
2023
Institution
Tribhuvan University, Institute of Engineering
Technologies
Financial Modeling Monte Carlo Simulation Excel Risk Analysis Real Estate Economics

This comprehensive case study analyzed the financial viability of investing in Classic Tower, a 167-unit commercial building in Hattiban, Kathmandu. As part of a team of four mechanical engineering students, we conducted an in-depth economic feasibility analysis from the perspective of a potential buyer, evaluating all financial aspects of the property investment.

Our analysis integrated various economic principles, financial calculations, and real estate market dynamics to guide investment decision-making. We examined revenue streams, operational costs, mortgage financing, and conducted sophisticated risk assessment using Monte Carlo simulation to evaluate the property's investment potential under various market conditions.

Methodology

Our analysis followed a structured approach:

  1. Data Collection: Gathered property specifics, market rates, and operating assumptions from Sams Management and other reliable sources.
  2. Revenue Projections: Calculated Potential Gross Revenue using Triple Net Lease contracts accounting for base rent, property taxes, insurance, and maintenance costs.
  3. Expense Analysis: Detailed all operating expenses including property management fees (5% of gross revenue), CAM charges (Rs. 5,000/unit/month), utilities, taxes, and insurance.
  4. Financial Modeling: Developed comprehensive financial models in Excel to calculate key metrics.
  5. Risk Assessment: Conducted Monte Carlo simulations (10,000 trials) to evaluate ROI under varying conditions.
  6. Sensitivity Analysis: Identified key variables impacting investment returns.

Simulation & Risk Analysis

We conducted extensive Monte Carlo simulations to evaluate investment risks under varying market conditions:

ROI Probability Distribution

  • Mean ROI: 8.29% with standard deviation of 2.28%
  • Best-case scenario: 17.65% ROI
  • Worst-case scenario: -0.27% ROI
  • 70% probability of achieving ROI > 7.06%

Sensitivity Analysis Findings

The ROI was found to be:

  • 76.8% sensitive to occupancy rate changes
  • 18.3% sensitive to lease amount variations
  • Minimally affected by interest rate and CAM changes

10-Year Profit Forecast

  • Mean total profit: NRS 860,968,725
  • Range: NRS 530,090,820 (worst) to NRS 1,209,131,618 (best)
  • 50% probability of profit between NRS 792,657,046 and NRS 928,334,015

Conclusions & Recommendations

Our analysis demonstrated that Classic Tower presents a financially viable investment opportunity with:

  • Strong fundamental metrics (8% Cap Rate, 12.68% ROI)
  • Healthy debt coverage (DCR 2.12)
  • Positive cash flow even in most downside scenarios

Key recommendations for potential investors:

  1. Focus on maintaining high occupancy rates (primary ROI driver)
  2. Implement strategic marketing to optimize lease rates without compromising occupancy
  3. Consider adding IRR and comparative analysis for more comprehensive evaluation
  4. Establish MARR (Minimum Attractive Rate of Return) benchmarks
