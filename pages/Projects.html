Financial Analysis of a commercial rental building in Nepal

A case study on the financial analysis of a commercial rental building in Nepal. The project involved data collection, analysis, and Monte Carlo simulation to assess the financial viability of the project. We calculated the ROI using standard rates and performed Monte Carlo Simulations to estimate the average net income and standard deviation based on different inputs. This analysis helped us provide recommendations on whether to purchase the property.
